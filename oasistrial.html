Chocolate Chip Cookies


Most traditional chocolate chip cookie recipes start by creaming softened butter with sugar. Here, the butter is melted in a skillet until golden brown and nutty first. This simple step does require a bit of extra time, but the result is an intensely toasty butterscotch note that makes these thin, chewy cookies irresistible.

https://www.foodandwine.com/recipes/brown-butter-chocolate-chip-cookies
